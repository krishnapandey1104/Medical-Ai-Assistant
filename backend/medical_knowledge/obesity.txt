Obesity Overview

Obesity is a medical condition characterized by excessive body fat accumulation that increases the risk of health problems.

Body Mass Index (BMI)

BMI is used to classify weight status.

Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9  
Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9  
Obesity: BMI 30 or higher

Health Risks

Heart disease  
Type 2 diabetes  
High blood pressure  
Joint problems  
Sleep apnea  
Certain cancers

Causes

High calorie diet  
Physical inactivity  
Genetic factors  
Hormonal imbalance  
Stress

Prevention and Management

Balanced diet  
Regular physical activity  
Healthy lifestyle habits  
Adequate sleep  
Regular medical monitoring
