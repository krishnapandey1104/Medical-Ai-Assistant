Thyroid Overview

The thyroid is a butterfly-shaped gland located in the neck. It produces hormones that regulate metabolism, energy production, and body temperature.

Common Thyroid Disorders

Hypothyroidism

Hypothyroidism occurs when the thyroid gland does not produce enough thyroid hormone.

Symptoms

Fatigue  
Weight gain  
Cold intolerance  
Dry skin  
Depression

Hyperthyroidism

Hyperthyroidism occurs when the thyroid gland produces too much thyroid hormone.

Symptoms

Weight loss  
Rapid heartbeat  
Anxiety  
Sweating  
Heat intolerance

Normal Thyroid Test Levels

TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone): 0.4 – 4.0 mIU/L

Diagnosis

Thyroid disorders are diagnosed using blood tests such as TSH, T3, and T4 levels.

Management

Treatment depends on the specific thyroid condition and should be supervised by healthcare professionals.
