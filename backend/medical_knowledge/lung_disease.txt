Lung Disease Overview

Lung diseases affect the respiratory system and interfere with breathing and oxygen exchange.

Common Lung Diseases

Asthma
Asthma causes inflammation and narrowing of airways, leading to breathing difficulty.

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
COPD includes chronic bronchitis and emphysema, usually caused by smoking.

Pneumonia
Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs.

Symptoms of Lung Disease

Shortness of breath  
Persistent cough  
Chest tightness  
Wheezing  
Fatigue  

Risk Factors

Smoking  
Air pollution  
Respiratory infections  
Occupational exposure to dust or chemicals

Prevention

Avoid smoking  
Maintain good air quality  
Exercise regularly  
Vaccinations for respiratory infections
