Vitamin Deficiency Overview

Vitamins are essential nutrients that support normal body functions. A deficiency occurs when the body does not receive enough of a specific vitamin.

Common Vitamin Deficiencies

Vitamin D Deficiency
Symptoms may include bone pain, muscle weakness, and increased risk of fractures.

Vitamin B12 Deficiency
May cause fatigue, nerve damage, and anemia.

Vitamin C Deficiency
Can lead to scurvy, fatigue, and poor wound healing.

Iron Deficiency
Often causes anemia and fatigue.

Symptoms of Vitamin Deficiency

Fatigue  
Weakness  
Hair loss  
Pale skin  
Frequent infections

Prevention

Balanced diet  
Eating fruits and vegetables  
Sunlight exposure for vitamin D  
Regular health checkups
